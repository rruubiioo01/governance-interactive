# Governance Interactive

## Project Overview

This project consists of six modules designed to enhance user engagement and provide essential tools for digital citizenship.

### Modules Description
1. **Legal Guardian Digital**: A module providing resources and tools for legal guardians to facilitate informed decision-making in the digital realm.
2. **Entiende tu Derecho**: This module educates users about their rights in the digital space, offering clear and accessible information.
3. **Reputación Verificada**: A feature focused on helping users understand and manage their digital reputation through verified information and tools.
4. **IA sin Filtros**: A module that delivers unfiltered access to Artificial Intelligence resources, promoting transparency and understanding of AI technologies.
5. **Testamento Digital**: This module assists users in creating a digital will, ensuring their online legacy is managed according to their wishes.
6. **Escáner de Exposición**: A tool designed to help users assess their online exposure and take necessary actions to safeguard their privacy.

## Features
- User-friendly interface
- Cross-platform accessibility
- Real-time updates on legal and digital rights
